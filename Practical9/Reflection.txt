Reflection:
This practical was truly challenging and rewarding. It is an application that translates abstract epidemiological concepts into concrete probabilistic model code.
SIR.py was relatively straightforward, while SIR_vaccination.py introduced and adjusted the parameter of vaccination rate. spatial_SIR.py was more difficult, and writing pseudocode in advance was truly helpful.
Stochastic models produce noticeably different results every time they run, which reflects the randomness inherent in the process of disease spread.
